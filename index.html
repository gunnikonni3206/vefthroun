Interstellar

cast

The main cast of Interstellar includes Matthew McConaughey as Joseph Cooper, a former NASA pilot; Anne Hathaway as Amelia Brand, a scientist and astronaut; Jessica Chastain as Murph, Cooper's daughter and a brilliant scientist; Bill Irwin as the voice of TARS, a robot assistant; and Michael Caine as Professor Brand, a scientist who devises the plan to save humanity.

Plot

When Earth becomes uninhabitable and humanity faces extinction, farmer and ex-NASA pilot Joseph Cooper (Matthew McConaughey) pilots a ship of scientists and engineers through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet in another galaxy.

critics

The film received critical acclaim for its screenplay, direction, themes, visual effects, and performances. It was a commercial success, grossing over $677 million worldwide. Interstellar received several awards and nominations, including five Academy Awards.

Interstellar has been praised for its scientific accuracy and has sparked conversations about space exploration, the environment, and the future of humanity.
