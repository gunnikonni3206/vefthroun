Runaway

"Runaway" is a 35-minute short film directed by Kanye West that was released in 2010 as a visual accompaniment to his fifth studio album, "My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy."

Phoenix's Journey

The film follows a phoenix who falls to earth and is discovered by Kanye's character, who then helps her navigate the human world and ultimately puts on a lavish feast to celebrate her arrival.
